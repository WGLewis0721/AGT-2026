Updated the AGT backend to the current balance-only SMS flow and merged the finished backend branch into `main`.

Current backend behavior
- Stripe sends `checkout.session.completed` to API Gateway.
- Lambda verifies the webhook signature.
- Lambda calculates the deposit paid and remaining balance due.
- Lambda sends:
  - a detailer SMS with booking details, deposit, and balance
  - a customer confirmation SMS with deposit confirmation and balance guidance
- No payment links are sent by SMS.

- Removed all pay-link logic from `backend-integration/lambda/lambda_function.py`.
- Removed:
  - `BALANCE_PAYMENT_LINK`
  - `shorten_url()`

  - `urllib.parse`
  - all `pay_link` references
- Kept:
  - `SERVICE_PRICES`
  - balance calculation logic
  - Stripe signature verification
  - structured CloudWatch logging
  - Textbelt SMS delivery
  - dev/prod environment separation

Current SMS behavior
- Detailer SMS now contains:
  - customer name, phone, email
  - service, date, location
  - deposit amount
  - balance amount when the service matches the price map
- Customer SMS now contains:
  - booking confirmation
  - service, date, location
  - deposit received
  - balance due after service when known
  - fallback text when the balance is not mapped yet
- No URLs appear in either SMS.
